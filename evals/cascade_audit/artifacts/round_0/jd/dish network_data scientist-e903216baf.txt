Job Description
Perform data engineering and analysis activities related to the building of the first standalone 5G network. Optimize the performance of DISH Wireless Products, Services, Processes & Programs. Determine Improvement Actions for Products, Services, Processes & Programs. Focus on Architecture, Testing, Quality, Data Science and AI/ML process areas & domains. Work with vendors to improve the performance of their products and services. Participate in vendor analysis and selection for DISH Wireless products, services, and support. Analyze and recommend improvements on DISH quality, governance, and transformation.
