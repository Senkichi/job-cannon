Description
QC Ware is seeking a
Staff Scientist in Quantum Machine Learning (QML)
to lead the development of novel quantum algorithms and hybrid quantum-classical models for real-world applications.
This role sits at the intersection of quantum computing, machine learning, and delivering real-world solutions for industrial and enterprise applications. It will contribute directly to QC Ware’s core research initiatives, software products, and customer-facing projects. This role reports directly to the VP of Quantum Algorithms.
We encourage candidates to apply via LinkedIn for the fastest review:
Apply here
‍
Role
Conduct research in quantum machine learning, focusing on novel quantum and hybrid quantum-classical algorithms
Develop and optimize algorithms for near-term quantum advantage
Design and implement quantum and hybrid machine learning models
Adapt classical machine learning techniques to quantum-enhanced frameworks
Evaluate model and algorithm performance through benchmarking and testing
Analyze trade-offs between classical, quantum-inspired, and quantum approaches
Collaborate with cross-functional teams including software engineering, quantum physics, and product teams
Contribute to integrating algorithms into QC Ware platforms
Document methodologies, experiments, and results
Prepare technical reports, research papers, and presentations, including customer-facing technical material
Identify high-impact problem instances and design end-to-end solution strategies bridging AI, optimization, and quantum computing
Minimum Qualifications
MSc in Computer Science, Physics, Machine Learning, Quantum Computing, or a related field (or equivalent experience)
1+ years of experience in quantum computing, quantum machine learning, research or advanced machine learning research
Strong understanding of quantum or quantum-inspired algorithms and their applications in machine learning
Proficiency in Python and scientific computing libraries
Experience with quantum computing frameworks (e.g., Qiskit, Cirq, PennyLane)
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
Excellent communication and collaboration abilities
Experience & Leveling Guidelines
Associate level: minimum ~1 year of relevant experience
Staff level: typically ~3+ years of relevant experience
These ranges reflect baseline expectations. Final leveling will depend on overall qualifications, including technical depth, research impact (e.g., publications, citations), industry experience, and customer-facing capabilities.
Preferred Skills
Experience with hybrid quantum-classical algorithms and applications spanning NISQ and early FTQC devices
Background in applied machine learning or data science, in particular generative models, GenAI, or agentic AI tools
Experience working on real-world or industry-facing use cases
Publications in quantum computing, quantum machine learning, or related fields
Experience collaborating across research, product, business, and customer-facing teams

About You
As a Staff Scientist in Quantum Machine Learning (QML) you will combine deep technical expertise with an applied mindset, helping translate cutting-edge research into practical solutions for near-term and emerging quantum hardware, including NISQ and early fault-tolerant quantum computing (early FTQC) devices.
We are particularly interested in candidates with experience in modern machine learning approaches, especially generative models, GenAI, and agentic AI systems.
About QC Ware
QC Ware is a quantum computing software company that builds quantum-inspired enterprise solutions and provides professional services to large enterprises. Our mission is to deliver practical quantum advantage by developing and deploying quantum and quantum-inspired solutions that create measurable value in real-world applications.
Backed by one of the world’s strongest teams of quantum algorithms scientists, QC Ware is headquartered in Palo Alto, CA, and works with leading global organizations.
